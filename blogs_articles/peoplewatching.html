People Watching

The wonders of life and people. Maybe cats too.

Humans are the most complex creatures in the animal kingdom, exhibiting behaviours that might leave animals bewildered. We walk on two feet, enjoy a vast array of foods, experiment with and consume plants, and travel in metal machines, among countless other activities. This rich tapestry of behaviours sets us apart from the more straightforward, predictable lives of other animals.

Take cats, for example. Their days revolve around eating, sleeping, and occasional bursts of activity. They might meow for food or attention, but their lives follow a simple and consistent pattern. In stark contrast, human lives are dynamic and varied. Our daily routines can shift dramatically, and we even rely on apps to help us stay organized.

Like cats, we crave attention, but we seek it in a multitude of ways: playing video games, socializing, posting on social media, and even skydiving. Our quest for attention is diverse and multifaceted. Humans are extraordinary beings, brimming with a wide range of emotions, thoughts, and behaviours. Our uniqueness springs from our individual experiences, upbringings, cultures, and genetics.

So, embrace your differences. Everyone around you is unique too.
