Design Brief

Members Involved: Me (Justin), Kong, Lutfiah.

To write, direct and produce a short promotional video about a product. We were given the choice to choose an existing product or make up our own! So we chose Spotify.

The Plan

Before settling on Spotify, we brainstormed to devise a plan for evenly distributing the workload and selecting a brand that we all enjoy. Additionally, we chose a strategy that would maximize the use of motion graphics instead of outdoor filming and shooting.

Objectives

  1. Moodboard of Motion Graphics.
  2. Consistency with Spotify Branding.
  3. Create a Script.
  4. Storyboard.
  5. AE Animation.
  6. Final Video.

Script

After discussion, we decided to split up the video into 3 sections. 1st was the intro done by Me, 2nd the rising action which is done by Kong and finally the 3rd part which is the resolution done by Lutfiah.

Fun Fact! As we couldn't figure out what to write for our script initially, we used ChatGPT with prompts to help us generate ideas to get us moving with the project. You can see the AI script in our miro board with the link below!

Storyboard

Started production and came up with the storyboard for our video! All 3 of us worked together to ensure our storyboards have a continuous flow and following the consistency amongst shots.

Final

After completeting our objectives, consultation, editing and final touch ups. We have created our end product which is the video above!
